About the Conference

Arab and Islamic societies encounter numerous contemporary challenges across various sectors. These challenges and obstacles hinder the development and advancement of these societies, and pose a significant impediment to achieving progress and prosperity. It has become crucial to clearly define these challenges, identify their risks, and explore methods for addressing and preventing them.

The need to discuss these issues is amplified by the rapid pace of technological development and advancement worldwide. Idleness in the face of these challenges only exacerbates the problems and difficulties facing these societies. Therefore, it is imperative to proactively examine these challenges and devise effective solutions.

This conference is a serious contribution to this important endeavor, opening avenues for researchers to contribute, to search, and explore these challenges, their risks, and solutions in light of rapid technological advancements and discoveries. This in turn will enable Arab and Islamic societies to keep pace with scientific and developmental progress and contribute to sustainable development using the latest contemporary technologies.
