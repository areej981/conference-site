About the Conference

Arab societies face numerous contemporary challenges in various fields. These challenges and obstacles represent serious barriers to development and progress, hindering the achievement of prosperity and advancement. It has become essential to identify and clearly define these challenges, understand their risks, and explore effective methods for addressing and preventing them.

The need to discuss these issues has become even more urgent in light of the rapid technological development and global digital transformation. Standing idle in the face of these challenges only worsens the problems and complexities confronting Arab societies. Therefore, proactive efforts must be undertaken to analyze these issues and propose effective solutions.

This conference represents a serious contribution within this important framework, opening new horizons for researchers to study, explore, and investigate these challenges, their risks, and their treatment methods in accordance with the accelerating technological advancements. This will help Arab societies keep pace with scientific and developmental progress and contribute effectively to sustainable development using the latest contemporary technologies.
