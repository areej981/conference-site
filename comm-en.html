Requirements for Submission of Abstracts and Research Papers

  1. The abstract and the research must address one of the conference's main themes or sub-themes, including the methodology, objectives, results, and recommendations, and should not exceed 250 words.
  2. Research papers within one of the themes will undergo a peer-review process by the conference's scientific committee.
  3. Research papers are accepted in both Arabic and English.
  4. The first page must include the conference name, the organizing body, the full title of the research paper, the researcher's name, academic degree, place of work, and email address.
  5. The research must adhere to scientific research methodology and be original.
  6. The research must not have been previously published or accepted for publication at another conference.
  7. The research paper should not exceed 20 pages, including references, images, and appendices.
  8. The research paper must be typed using Microsoft Word.
  9. The font should be Times New Roman.
  10. Research title: 14 pt bold; subheadings: 12 pt bold.
  11. The abstract must be written in both Arabic and English.
  12. All papers will be subject to plagiarism checks and an AI-based plagiarism detection system, with a maximum similarity rate of 25%.
  13. Accepted conference papers will be published in a special issue.

Second: Conference Proceedings

Research papers presented at the conference sessions will be published in the Conference Proceedings Book in PDF format.
