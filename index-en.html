The organizing body is honored to invite you to participate in the First International Peer-Reviewed Scientific Conference entitled "Contemporary Challenges of Arab Societies and Strategies for Addressing Them in Light of Modern Technology", which aims to diagnose the major challenges facing Arab societies and propose practical solutions benefiting from rapid technological development.

We welcome the participation of faculty members, researchers, and postgraduate students from universities, research centers, and related institutions.

Conference Goals & Themes

Conference Goals

  • Highlight major challenges facing Arab societies.
  • Provide a platform for researchers to present scientific solutions.
  • Enhance cooperation between universities and research institutions.
  • Utilize modern technology in solving societal issues.

Selected Themes

  • Legal and Legislative Challenges
  • Social, Educational and Psychological Challenges
  • Cultural, Intellectual and Islamic Civilization Challenges
  • Media and Social Media Challenges
  • Arabic Language and Literature Challenges
  • Cultural and Technological Challenges
  • Economic and Financial Challenges
  • Applied Natural Sciences Challenges
  • Youth and Women Challenges
  • Management and Business Challenges
  • Health and Nursing Sector Challenges
