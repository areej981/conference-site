Conference Invitation

The organizing body is honored to invite you to participate in the First International Peer-Reviewed Scientific Conference entitled "Contemporary Challenges of Arab Societies and Strategies for Addressing Them in Light of Modern Technology", which aims to identify the most significant challenges faced by Arab societies and propose practical solutions benefiting from rapid technological advancement.

Participation is open to faculty members, researchers, and postgraduate students from universities, research centers, and relevant governmental and private institutions.

Conference Goals & Selected Themes

Conference Objectives

  • High lightening the most prominent challenges encountering Arab and Islamic societies.
  • Providing a platform for researchers to write and search these challenges and propose appropriate solutions.
  • Taking advantage from the experiences of other nations and peoples in solving their problems.
  • Strengthening partnerships between academic and scientific institutions in the Arab and Islamic world in discussing issues of concern to Arab and Islamic societies.
  • Utilizing modern technologies to address the contemporary challenges facing Arab and Islamic societies.
  • Actively contributing to achieve sustainable development by using the latest technological advancements.

Selected Themes

  • Legal and Legislative Challenges.
  • Social, Educational, and Psychological Challenges.
  • Cultural, Intellectual and Islamic Civilization Challenges
  • Media and Social Media Challenges
  • Arabic Language and Literature Challenges
  • Cultural and Technological Challenges
  • Economic and Financial Challenges.
  • Applied Natural Sciences Challenges
  • Youth and Women Challenges.
  • Management and Business Challenges.
  • Health and Nursing Sector Challenges.

* Additional detailed themes can be added on a separate page if desired.
